How it works

1. Pick your items and view basket

Browse our online menu and add the items you want to order to your basket. When you’re ready view your basket to check out

2. Choose a delivery method

We offer store pick-up from all our stores and deliver to a range of postcodes both in the North East and Manchester

3. Stay updated by email or text

Once you’ve chosen a time and date for your order we’ll prepare your food and let you know when it's out for delivery or ready to pick up

4. Enjoy your food!

When your delivery arrives we’ll place it on your doorstep contact-free. For store pick-up we’ll ask to see the code in your confirmation email

Delivery costs & zones

Our North East kitchen deliveres Mon - Sun, 9am - 8:30pm to the postcodes shown below:

Postcode Delivery cost
NE1 £1.50
NE2 - NE4 £2.00
NE5 - NE12 £2.50
NE15 & NE21 £2.50

The minimum order value accepted is £12.00 (Free delivery for orders above £25).

COVID-19 safe / Contact free delivery

Your delivery driver will knock, place your order at your door, call and wait nearby to make sure you receive it.

Cash payment is unavailable in order to maintain government recommended distancing.
